Info

Reports are created by report automations.

Daily reports include CVEs collected during the previous full day in the automation’s timezone.

Weekly reports include CVEs collected during the previous 7 full days, ending the day before the scheduled send time.

For example, a weekly report scheduled on Friday includes CVEs from the previous Friday through Thursday.
